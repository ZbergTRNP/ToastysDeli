About

Welcome to Toasty's Deli, located in historic downtown Gainesville, Ga

Welcome to Toasty's Deli

We began as a little shop, but over the years, we've expanded to become a well-liked hangout for sandwich fans. We are a locally owned offering consistently delicious food prepared from scratch.


Our Purpose: At Toasty's Deli, we believe that a good sandwich can brighten anyone's day. We are dedicated to producing tasty, premium sandwiches for everyone for this reason. We firmly believe in fostering a warm and friendly environment for our customers.


Our Values: We have dedicated ourselves to making sure that our sandwiches are made with only the freshest, best-quality ingredients. Because of this, we strive to get our fruits, vegetables, meats, and cheeses from local, sustainable farms. Additionally, we support environmental responsibility and use eco-friendly materials in our packaging.


Our Sandwiches: We take pride in providing a variety of sandwich alternatives to satisfy every palate. Everyone may find something they enjoy here, from traditional cold cuts to inventive vegetarian and vegan options.
